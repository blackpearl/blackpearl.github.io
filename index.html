The Architect Who Speaks Both Languages

In a world where most architects operate on one side of the legacy-modern divide, I live in the intersection. My career began deep in COBOL/CICS mainframe engineering and has evolved into leading enterprise cloud transformations on AWS — a combination that is genuinely rare in the Canadian insurance technology market.

In my current organization, I serve as a Solutions Architect deriving modern solutions for enterprise policy administration systems. Also, I participate in Pre-Sales engagements — drafting SOWs, performing effort estimations, and building ROI narratives that turn complex technical capabilities into business decisions.

I'm actively deepening my AI/ML expertise across multiple institutions — including the highly regarded University of Toronto — by building hands-on mini use cases purpose-built for the insurance industry, from claims automation to GenAI-driven legacy analysis. My target next step is a Solutions Architect or Director of Technology role where I can shape strategy at the intersection of modernization, cloud, and AI for Financial instituations.
